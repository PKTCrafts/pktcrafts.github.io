Welcome!

My name is Per, and I’m the creator behind PKT Crafts. I’m passionate about making dioramas and a variety of other creative projects.

My YouTube channel is where I share my love for crafting, with the hope of not only entertaining you but also inspiring you to create something of your own.

I enjoy experimenting with different styles and materials, always challenging myself to bring new ideas to life in miniature form.

Whether you’re here for the artistry or just curious about the process, I hope my work sparks your imagination!
